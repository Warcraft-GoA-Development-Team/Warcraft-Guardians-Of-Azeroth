#####################
Features
* Ankoan can now appear in the courts of non-Ankoan followers of similar religions, such as Waterspeaker, Water Deities and Neptulon.
* Around in-game year 610, the Wandering Isle's existence once again becomes known to the public. This event opens a possibility for pandaren from the Isle to appear in various courts in Azeroth, provided you're not a follower of an evil religion. This event also precedes a bigger discovery that happens shortly after...
* Added a brand new crisis, the Amathet Invasion.
* Added the Suntouched heresy for the Titanic religion. Amathet and Lost City (aka Asova) provinces are now Suntouched, along with the local Sethir dynasty. The Suntouched allows the priest class and the Titanic branches.
* Added Uldum Cloaker event, which should fire on a decade pulse, starting from year 600, optimally should fire near 600 in first bookmark and 610 in latter two. This option is also regulated by a gamerule and can be disabled.
* Added events for the Dark Pharaoh (Tekahn) that can fire if a character owns the county of Neferset, is a Tol'vir and a Shadow priest and Uldum is open to everyone.
* Added two Tol'vir mercenary companies: Tah-Ankhat and Tah-Bosari.
* Added Yak Statue wonder for yaungol characters instead of Horse Statue.
* Added the Band of the Ranger General artefact for its historical owners.

Interface
* Added custom portraits for:
   * Eredar
   * Draenei
   * Dwarves
* Added naga clothes.
* Added naga holding icons.
* Updated Windrunner, Thaurissan, Wildhammer, Bronzebeard, Sunstrider CoAs.
* If you can't declare wars because of isolation restrictions, it's shown in the CB tooltip.
* Hid Random and Shattered World buttons.

Tweaks
* The Isle of Thunder (a tropical swamp) is no longer covered by snowfall.

Balance
* Added Sky Burial for yaungol that can be used at any time regardless of religion if playing that race.

Bug Fixes
* Fixed bug where mind control decision checked if the target was its own courtier or vassal.
* Fixed an issue where the Frostwolf's reaction to the Invasion from Draenor wasn't localized.
* Fixed an issue where the Pandaria AI could join the Horde or Alliance.
* Lei Shen is spawned with the Immortal trait now.
* Fixed an issue where characters with the Brahmin trait could appear.
* Fixed an issue where you could invite orcish clans with the Dark Portal being destroyed.
* Added elemental names for the flamewakers.
* Fixed an issue where fel troll children had no portraits.
* The qiraji always pick C'Thun as the main Old God, since they're sealed with him.

#
#####################
Features
* The continent of Pandaria has made it to the game, with its cultures, religions and features like:
   * Rulers located in Pandaria may become infested by Sha if they have negative traits like Wroth, Depressed, Paranoid, Craven, Zealous, Proud, Cruel.
   * The mogu can use the decision Build New Vessel if they are too old, are maimed, or have negative genetic traits to create a new body. You will be rejuvenated and lose all body-related traits.
   * Pandaria is isolated. Pandarian characters can't declare wars, send councillors, build trade posts, or marry someone outside of Pandaria. The Mists of Pandaria parts around the year 613. A game rule to regulate the Mists is included.
   * The Pandaria religions have Taoist minor titles.
   * The Chi religion group can use the decision to Choose a Chi School.
   * The Chi religion group can use Subjugate CB.
   * The Chi religion group can Go on a Pilgrimage if they pick the Theology Focus.
   * The Chi religion group has Buddhist ambitions to remove sin traits.
   * The Chi religion group can Search for a Guru.
   * All Pandaria cultures except the Mantid can restore the Pandarian Empire (the name depends on the culture of the character) via the decisions.
   * Added the Heart of Y'Shaarj. It can be used to infest characters with Sha.
   * The Zandalari can get an event to remember Lei Shen's promise and claim Kun Lai if the Mists of Pandaria have parted. The event fires a few days after the Mists are gone. Also, it may fire randomly during the game.
   * Lei Shen can be resurrected by the Shenic religion and the Zandalari (if they remember Lei Shen's promise).
   * Every ten years, the Klaxxi choose the best character within the Mantid culture to become a paragon. The Paragons get the Paragon trait, bloodline, and are put into a deep slumber, but can still be awakened by right-clicking later.
   * Added the Monk class.
* Old Gods:
   * You can awaken the Old Gods. The awakened Old Gods are represented by an upgrade in these wonders: C'Thun in Ahn'qiraj, Yogg-Saron in Ulduar, N'Zoth in the Shrine of Storms.
   * The Old Gods use different states and policies now. The awakened Old Gods give more troops and more powerful generals.
   * You need a Fully Free Old God to restore the Black Empire now.
   * You can now become the Twilight of the Gods if you kill all the Old Gods.
   * The Leader of Twilight's Hammer can forge the Hammer of Twilight during the Cataclysm.
* Generic artefacts:
   * Added generic ranged artefacts.
   * Added a chance to receive generic artefacts after completing a society quest. Added the Society Artefact Rewards game rule to regulate this.
   * Characters with higher title tiers will have an increased chance to receive high tier artefacts. In comparison, characters with lower title tiers will have an increased opportunity to receive low tier artefacts on the game start. Added the Game Start Artifacts Rarity game rule to regulate this.
   * Various factors, like culture, affect chances of getting specific types of artefacts. Added the Artifacts Preferences game rule to regulate this.
   * Replaced random TOG looting events with a random event that spawns artefacts from the mod after a successful raid. A game rule to regulate how often this event is fired included.
* Added a unique wonder, Janeiro's Point, to Booty Bay province.

Interface
* Added customised portraits for:
   * Pandaren
   * Yinyu
   * Saurok
   * Hozen
   * Trolls
   * Yaungol
   * Mantid
   * Grummle
   * Mogu
* Added a few flags all over the world.
* The Chi religion group uses Chi as piety.
* Added static portraits for Lor'themar.
* Added triggers to many static portraits so that the characters don't look normal when they become a demon or undead.

Tweaks
* Sylvanas is the Ranger-General of Quel'thalas in the year 603, her mother, Lireesa in the 583rd year and Halduron in the 605th year.
* The artefact game rules now belong to a separate group.

Balance
* Evil characters should have less default personal traits.
* Independent AI rulers are less likely to do evil actions like converting to one of the evil religions: Sargerite, Death God, etc.
* Increased military techs in Kul Tiras, Tol Barad, Gilneas and Mechagon.
* Increased military techs in Plunderer Isle after the year 600.

Bug Fixes
* Fixed the Lightbound heresy icon having a wolven head as an icon.

* A couple of dozen other localisation fixes.

* Troll provinces in the Hinterlands don't have castles and cities except for Jintha'alor.
* Fixed an issue where a Horde vassal got a negative opinion of the Horde leader when she/he (Horde vassal) gives her/his titles away.

* Fixed an issue where the Mind Control event chain didn't stop on your target's death.
* Fixed an issue where the Disembowel necromancer's decision could raise suspicions if you were in the Alchemists' Union society.
* Lor'themar has one eye and the Scarred trait in the 605th bookmark.
